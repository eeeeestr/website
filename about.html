about me

hi! i'm bubbles. i hate writing about me pages, but i love reading them, so i'll put some effort. i started messing around with computers since i have memory. the first time i got into linux i was 11 years old i think, and i remember managing to install arch linux with this tutorial. ever since i've been a slave of the penguin.

despite that, i still have no idea how to code or properly write a shellscript. i have tried programming and scripting but all the syntax and logic components just don't stick in my head, i bet it's due to lack of practice, though.

i was talking with coworker during some free time we had at work, and they asked me if i had any passion projects. anything that got me going "damn, i wanna get home and do this!". and i awkwardly smiled and said that i don't think i do. that's how this website began. i've tried making multiple websites in the past. this one is still active as time of writing this, but i lost my ssh key and cannot access my account now (whoops).
